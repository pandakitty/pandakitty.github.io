The Bridge Between Healthcare and Data

Transitioning from the high-stakes world of clinical data management to predictive analytics has been a journey of precision and purpose.

"My journey is defined by a transition from high-stakes clinical data management to advanced predictive modeling. I apply the same 'zero-discrepancy' mindset learned in the NICU to modern business intelligence challenges."

In the NICU, I learned that every data point represents a person. Today, I use that same meticulous attention to detail to ensure that business insights are accurate, actionable, and ethical.
